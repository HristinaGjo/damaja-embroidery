Stefanija Pejchinovska, who works behind the name Damaja, is a hand embroiderer based in Berlin, Germany. She is a graduated architect who finds ultimate pleasure in hand embroidery as meditative and satisfying craft.

Stefanija is self taught and practice hand embroidery in the past two decades. She believes in more mindful approach to life through reuse of textiles, slow fashion, slow creation. Her work consists of teaching workshops, art embroidery explorations and conscious collaborations with local designers and ethical brands.
